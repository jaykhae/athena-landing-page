How do I Sell E-Funds on Athena Protocol?

1. Visit our Home Page
2. Click on Launch
3. Initiate contact with an Athena Agent
4. Indicate to the agent you will like to place a Sell Order for E-Fund
5. Specify the E-Fund asset and amount
6. Make payment to the provided tag
7. Provide fiat account or crypto wallet to receive funds
