OTC Trading

OTC Trading stands for "Over-the-Counter" Trading.
Why use OTC Trading?

In OTC trading, transactions are negotiated directly between buyers and sellers, also giving flexibility by allowing both parties to trade Unlisted Asset Pairs.
How do Trade OTC on Athena Protocol?

1. Visit our Home Page
2. Click on Launch
3. Initate contact with an Athena Agent
4. Indicate you will like to start an OTC trade
5. Be clear and detailed on the terms and nature of the trade
6. Indicate the type of order, asset name and amount.
7. After both parties come to agreement the trade will commence.
