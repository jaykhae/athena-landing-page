TRC-20

TRC-20 is a token standard on the TRON blockchain. Similar to ERC-20 on Ethereum and BEP-20 on Binance Smart Chain, TRC-20 defines a set of rules and functionalities for tokens on the TRON network.



How do I buy TRC-20 tokens on Athena Protocol?

1. Visit our Home Page
2. Click on Launch
3. Initate contact with an Athena Agent
4. Get a price quote based amount of TRC-20 tokens needed
5. Send receiving TRC-20 Wallet Address
6. Make Payment for TRC-20 token Order


How do I sell TRC-20 tokens on Athena Protocol?

1. Visit our Home Page
2. Click on Launch
3. Initate contact with an Athena Agent
4. Specify amount of crypto you wish to sell
5. Specify the Chain/Network of the asset
6. Make payment to the provided address
7. Provide fiat account to receive funds
