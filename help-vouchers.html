What are Vouchers?

Vouchers are essentially certificates or tokens that represent a certain monetary value or entitlements to a specific product or service. They are often used as a form of payment by businesses. They can come in various forms including paper reciepts or digital codes that can be redeemed online or in store.

How to Redeem Vouchers

1. Visit our Home Page
2. Click on Launch
3. Initiate contact with an Athena Agent
4. Indicate to the agent you will like to Redeem Voucher
5. Specify the Voucher and the amount
6. Send a clear picture of the reciept showing the code or tracking number of the voucher
7. Choose mode of payment (Cash or Crypto)
8. Provide fiat account or Crypto Wallet to receive funds
