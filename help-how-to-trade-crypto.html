How to Trade Crypto

Trading cryptocurrencies involves buying and selling with the aim of making a profit. Here's a general guide on how to trade crypto.


DYOR (Do Your Own Research)

Before diving into crypto trading, it's crucial to understand the basics of blockchain technology, different cryptocurrencies, market trends, and trading strategies. There are plenty of online resources, courses, and communities where you can learn about crypto trading.


How do I Buy Crypto on Athena Protocol?

1. Visit our Home Page
2. Click on Launch
3. Initiate contact with an Athena Agent
4. Indicate to the agent you will like to place a Buy Order for crypto
5. Choose the crypto assets and select the blockchain network
6. Get a price quote based amount of crypto needed
7. Send receiving Wallet Address
8. Make Payment for Buy Order


How do I Sell Crypto on Athena Protocol?

1. Visit our Home Page
2. Click on Launch
3. Initiate contact with an Athena Agent
4. Indicate to the agent you will like to place a Sell Order for crypto
5. Specify the crypto asset and amount
6. Specify the Chain/Network of the asset
7. Make payment to the provided address
8. Provide fiat account to receive funds


Risk Mangement

Crypto markets can be highly volatile, so it's essential to practice risk management techniques. Only invest what you can afford to lose, diversify your portfolio to limit potential losses.
